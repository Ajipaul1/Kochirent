Cost of Living in Kochi (2026)

Kochi remains one of Kerala's most balanced cities for people who want metro-level convenience without metro-level rent. A single professional typically budgets ₹22,000 to ₹38,000 monthly excluding high-end lifestyle spend. For couples, monthly expense often ranges ₹38,000 to ₹62,000 depending on rent and commute style. Families in 2 BHK apartments may budget ₹55,000+ based on school and transport choices.
